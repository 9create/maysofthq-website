About May Software Solutions

A Nagpur-based digital products company building focused software for the world's overlooked industries.

Who We Are
Built from the ground up, in Nagpur.

May Software Solutions is a registered MSME digital products company based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We design and develop software products for industries that are consistently overlooked by mainstream technology companies — independent educators, local healthcare providers, food safety advocates, and vernacular communities.

We are not a software agency. We build our own software products — focused, independent tools that we own, maintain, and improve. Each product serves a specific industry and solves a specific real-world problem.

"The problems we solve are not glamorous. They don't trend on Product Hunt or get covered in TechCrunch. But for the teacher, the doctor, the local contractor trying to run their business better — these problems are also one of the most important in the world."

Our Mission
Affordable. Focused. Built for the overlooked.
Build for the overlooked
The world's best software is built for Fortune 500 companies. We build for the ones they ignore — the teacher, the doctor, the lawyer, the electrician, the local contractor, the first-generation entrepreneur..
Keep it affordable
Affordable doesn't mean cheap — it means priced for the people who actually need it. Our products are built for real budgets, not venture-funded pricing experiments.
Vernacular first
Most of the world doesn't think in English. Our products are built with local language users at the center — not as an afterthought, but as the foundation.
